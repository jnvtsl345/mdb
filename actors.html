Christoph Waltz

Christoph Waltz was born in Vienna, Austria, to Elisabeth Urbancic, an Austrian-born costume designer, and Johannes Waltz, a German-born stage builder.

Born:October 4, 1956 in Vienna, Austria
Movies: Django Unchained, Horrible Bosses 2

Jonah Hill

Jonah Hill was born and raised in Los Angeles, the son of Sharon (Chalkin), a fashion designer and costume stylist, and Richard Feldstein, a tour accountant for Guns N' Roses.

Born:December 20, 1983 in Los Angeles, California, USA
Movies: This is the End, 21 Jump Street

Benedict Cumberbatch

Benedict Timothy Carlton Cumberbatch was born and raised in London, England. His parents, Wanda Ventham and Timothy Carlton are both actors.

Born:July 19, 1976 in Hammersmith, London, England, UK
Movies: The Imitation Game

Chris Pratt

Christopher Michael Pratt is an American film and television actor. He was born in Virginia, Minnesota, to Kathleen Louise (Indahl), who worked at a supermarket, and Daniel C. Pratt, who remodeled houses.

Born:June 21, 1979 in Virginia, Minnesota, USA
Movies: Guardians of the Galaxy, Rango

Johnny Depp

Johnny Depp is perhaps one of the most versatile actors of his day and age in Hollywood. He was born John Christopher Depp II in Owensboro, Kentucky, on June 9, 1963, to Betty Sue (Wells), who worked as a waitress, and John Christopher Depp, a civil engineer.

Born:June 9, 1963 in Owensboro, Kentucky, USA
Movies: Rango, Public Enemies

Will Ferrell

Will Ferrell was born in Irvine, California, to Betty Kay (Overman), a teacher, and Roy Lee Ferrell, Jr., a musician. His parents were both originally from Roanoke Rapids, North Carolina. A graduate of the University of Southern California, Ferrell became interested in performing while a student at University High School in Irvine.

Born:July 16, 1967 in Irvine, California, USA
Movies: The Other Guys, The Lego Movie
